Stockholm's oldest surviving building is The Riddarholmen Church, a Franciscan monastery built in 1270.

The city's subway is also known as the world's longest art gallery, with the majority of its stations being adorned with paintings, sculptures and mosaics.

The city is sometimes referred to as 'Venice of the North', thanks to its beautiful buildings and exquisite architecture, abundant open water and numerous parks.

TStockholm was built on 14 islands, connected by 57 bridges, earning the Swedish capital the nickname "Beauty on the Water".

During the second half of June it barely ever gets dark in Stockholm during some weeks due to the midnight sun.

Sweden has given the world some of the greatest pop bands and singers, including Abba, The Cardigans and Roxette.
